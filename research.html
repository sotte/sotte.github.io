Research

Between 2013 and 2015 I was a PhD student / researcher at the Machine Learning and Robotics lab (MLR) at the University Stuttgart, Germany. My supervisor was Prof. Marc Toussaint.

I worked on combining machine learning and robotics to enable robots to explore and learn the world efficiently.

The Curious Robot is a poster that I presented at the Machine Learning Summer School Tübingen in 2013.
